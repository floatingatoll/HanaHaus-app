HanaHaus is a public cafe open to all, located in the historic "New Varsity Theater" on palo Alto's main street. In addition to world class coffee and a selection of artisanal nourishing food for breakfast lunch and dinner, HanaHaus provides unique services and facilities for creative individuals in the entrepreneurial community to come together to WORK, PLAY and LEARN.

HanaHaus builds on the tradition of cafe culture worldwide, where a spirited and free exchange of ideas amongst members of a community has played a vital role in enriching our artistic, scientific and political and cultural life.

We have amazing partners that currently work with us and we're always happy to discuss ways in which we can grow the community.

Our Technology Desk, dubbed Techiatto, provides a space for technology platform companies to evangelize and support entrepreneurs looking to grow.
